How to find our office:

Doctors Medical Group of Colorado Springs is located at 3210 N. Academy Blvd, Suite 3 in Colorado Springs, CO.

We are south of North Carefree and north of Maizeland off of Academy Blvd but, unfortunately, you will not actually see our building on Academy Blvd itself.

Turn west onto Parkmoor Village Drive from Academy Blvd.

Turn north (right) into the driveway for the Academy Medical Arts buildings and head up the rise in the parking area until you reach the level parking in front of the 3210 building to your left.

(As you enter the parking area, you will pass the 3208 building on your left, you will see the 3220 building in front of you on your right, and finally see the 3210 building on your left.)
