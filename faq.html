Insurances we accept
We are happy to accept most commercial insurance plans at DMGCS. Here are the details.
Insurance Plans Accepted at DMGCS*
  • Aetna
  • Ameriben
  • Anthem
  • Blue Cross/Blue Shield
  • Cigna
  • Humana, Humana Gold/Medicare
  • Kaiser Permanente (NOT KP Select)
  • Mailhandlers
  • Medicare (we "do not accept assignment" for office visits but will bill medicare for you to receive reimbursement)
  • Medical Network
  • Colorado Medicaid for Consultation for Developmental-Behavioral Pediatrics with an authorized referral
  • Rocky Mountain Health Plans
  • Tricare West Region (UHC) up to age 18 years only (except secondary to Medicare)
  • Standard - up to age 18
  • Prime - authorized referral for Consultation for Developmental-Behavioral Pediatrics up to age 18
  • Tricare Secondary to Medicare Primary
  • United Healthcare for children through age 17 years only
  • *Please call if you do not see your health insurance plan on this list, thanks!
Insurance Plans NOT Accepted at DMGCS
  • CHP+
  • KP Select (Kaiser Select)
  • Medicaid as primary insurance for general primary care (we do accept Medicaid secondary to other insurance plans includeing Medicare)
  • Medicare administered by AARP, Evercare, Pacificare, Secure Horizons, United Healthcare
  • PHCS
  • United Healthcare ages 18 and older
